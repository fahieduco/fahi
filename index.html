About Me

Hiba Khudsia Siddiqua

With over a decade of experience in luxury Arabic calligraphy and engraving, I specialize in creating bespoke handwritten pieces and precision engravings for elite brands and discerning clients across Saudi Arabia.

My work has graced products and events for Montblanc, Swarovski, Rituals, Sephora, Bath & Body Works, and other prestigious brands, bringing an unparalleled level of artistry and craftsmanship to every project.

Based in Khobar but serving clients throughout the Eastern Province and across KSA, I combine traditional Islamic calligraphy techniques with contemporary aesthetics to create timeless pieces that elevate any brand experience.
